We believe every AI system should be auditable, explainable, and fair. bAIsed is the open-source toolkit that makes that possible.

Our Mission

AI systems are making high-stakes decisions in hiring, lending, healthcare, and criminal justice — often without accountability. Historical prejudices encoded in training data become automated systemic failures.

bAIsed exists to give every team — from startups to governments — the scientific tools to detect, quantify, and fix algorithmic bias before it causes real-world harm.
